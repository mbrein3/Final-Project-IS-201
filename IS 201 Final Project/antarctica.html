About Antarctica

Antarctica is the coldest, driest, and windiest continent on Earth. It contains nearly 70% of the planet’s fresh water locked in ice and plays a major role in regulating global climate.

Key Facts

  1. Geography
    • Located at the South Pole
    • Covered by ice year-round
    • Holds 70% of Earth’s fresh water
  2. Wildlife
    • Emperor Penguins
    • Weddell Seals
    • Antarctic Krill
